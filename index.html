SCHLOSS

Overview

Schloss is a NYC based DJ and founder of Not So Static, an events brand focused on selector-driven lineups and curated concepts. His sets blend technical precision with a selector’s taste for forward sounds that give each experience its own identity. Schloss plays house sets across minimal, afro, nu disco, and garage. Shaped by his time in Europe and years immersed in nightlife and music culture, he brings expertise as both a DJ and an event architect.
